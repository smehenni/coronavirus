Coviz is a collection of data visualizations around the COVID-19 pandemic. Data are collected from the Canadian government Public Health website, the Quebec government coronavirus page, and the Data Repository by Johns Hopkins CSSE.

Charts are automated using Datawrapper. You can download all datasets (in .csv) by clicking the "Get the data" link below visualizations.
